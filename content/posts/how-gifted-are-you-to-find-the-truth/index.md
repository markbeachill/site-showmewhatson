**irmak** 5 May 2020

In _The Gift_, a Turkish drama fantasy series, Atiye is an artist compelled to draw the same mystery symbol over and over again.

Unable to rest, she must throw off her current life and go on a journey to uncover her family’s secrets and understand what the symbol means.

The symbol is linked to her grandmother in some way, but her mother seems determined to block her from finding the truth. Is this a family curse? 

Atiye comes from a wealthy family and is about to marry to a seemingly perfect man. But something is not quite right about him and is she only really ready to marry for her parents’ approval?

Our heroine, naturally, breaks out of this trap and begins on her journey, beginning at an ancient archaeological dig. Has this symbol been seen before? How does it connect to her family’s past?

![](images/20200507_173917.jpg)

Might the symbol, something like a sand timer, be connected to the “deq” Kurdish tattoos used to protect against evil spirits?

How is Atiye’s grandmother connected? She is covered in these tattoos and some believe she has special powers and greet her by kissing her hands and clothes. 

Atiye’s journey, we are sure, will teach her not just about her family but also about herself. 

Is she fated to follow her family’s past? Should she keep digging? Where will it all end?

One thing is clear; Atiye’s passion and determination are inspirational. 

If you are intrigued, then what are you waiting for?

**Available on**: Netflix

**Genre**: Drama Fantasy

**Running Time:** 45 minutes an episode, 8 episodes
